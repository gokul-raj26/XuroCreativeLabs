# Google Search Console Setup Guide

## Step-by-Step Guide to Submit Your SEO Pages

### Phase 1: Verify Your Domain in Google Search Console

1. **Go to Google Search Console**
   - Visit: https://search.google.com/search-console/about
   - Sign in with your Google account

2. **Add Your Property**
   - Click "Add property"
   - Enter your domain: `xurocreativelabs.in`
   - Choose URL prefix option

3. **Verify Ownership**
   - Google will give you verification options:
     - HTML file (recommended)
     - DNS record
     - Google Analytics connection
   - Follow the verification method

---

### Phase 2: Submit Your Sitemap

1. **Go to Sitemaps Section**
   - In Google Search Console left sidebar
   - Click "Sitemaps"

2. **Submit New Sitemap**
   - Click "Add a new sitemap"
   - Enter: `xurocreativelabs.in/sitemap.xml`
   - Click "Submit"

3. **Verify Submission**
   - Wait 5-10 minutes
   - Status should show "Success"

---

### Phase 3: Monitor Your SEO Pages

1. **Check Indexing Status**
   - Go to "Coverage" section
   - Monitor how many pages are indexed
   - Look for any errors

2. **Track Keyword Rankings**
   - Go to "Performance"
   - You'll see impressions and clicks for your keywords
   - Takes 1-2 weeks to show data

3. **Check for Crawl Issues**
   - Go to "Coverage" 
   - Fix any errors that appear

## FAQ: Google Search Console

**Q: How long until my pages show up in Google?**
A: Usually 1-2 weeks for initial indexing. Ranking can take 2-6 months depending on competition.

**Q: My pages aren't indexed yet. What do I do?**
A: In GSC, click "Inspect URL" for each page and request indexing.

**Q: How often should I check GSC?**
A: Daily in first week after launch, then weekly. After 3 months, weekly or bi-weekly.

**Q: What's a good click-through rate?**
A: Depends on position. Position 1: 30-40%, Position 3: 10-15%, Position 10: 2-3%.

**Q: Should I buy an SEO tool?**
A: Start free. If you need to track 50+ keywords, consider SEMrush or Ahrefs.
